Bezpieczeństwo teleinformatyczne organizacji pozarządowych

Celem projektu Cert NGO jest wspieranie organizacji pozarządowych w budowaniu świadomości cyfrowej oraz reagowaniu na zagrożenia dla bezpieczeństwa systemów informatycznych.
help@cert.ngo

The Cert NGO project's goal is to support non-governmental organisations in building security awareness and responding to cybersecurity threats. Our aim is also to help NGOs protect their data and provide support in the event of an attack.

As part of the Cert NGO project, we offer the following support free of charge to non-governmental organisations:

  • network and information systems security monitoring
  • security audits
  • system and network penetration testing
  • IT security threat detection system
  • consultations and training for employees of the organisation
  • support in handling incidents, including mitigating impact and post-incident review
  • support in contacting offices responsible for the protection of personal data
  • services in the field of forensic IT and securing evidence material
  • support in threat modelling and designing processes aiming at its prevention or reduction

Please contact us at help@cert.ngo

Due to limited resources and the current situation in Ukraine, we cannot help all interested organisations. Therefore, we prioritise NGOs promoting human rights, anti-discrimination, and supporting refugees.
